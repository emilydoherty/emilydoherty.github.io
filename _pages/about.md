As a mixed-methods Ph.D. researcher in Computer Science and Cognitive Science, my work studies how people interact with generative AI systems in collaborative settings. I combine behavioral, linguistic, and cognitive methods to inform the design of responsible AI tools that support critical thinking, reflection, and everyday workflows.
